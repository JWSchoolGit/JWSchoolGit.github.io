Physical Activity in Your Life

What role does physical activity play in your life? Do you get enough movement each week? How does activity affect your mood, energy, and stress?
Physical activity is a way to improve yourself and get stronger. I think I get a decent amount of movement every week, however on the weekends I don't really do much, so probably not much movement. Activity can affect your mood, energy and stress by forcing your body to use its resources and energy to do your task.
